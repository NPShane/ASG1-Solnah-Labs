    Solnah Labs: the History


    In 2016, NP graduate Sehna set up his own game company with two of his longtime friends: Micheael and Juen-Sing. All three of them had yearned for the creative freedom that came with running their own company, and were daring enough to take the risk despite having no prior experience managing a studio and developing their own games.


    The first few years were hard and motivation among the three was low. They solidered on to develop their own unique mark on the gaming industry with their new IP, then called NeonFPS. However, 2020 brought much success for the team. Alongside NeonFPS, Solnah Labs had been brought on to help with external projects such as Strikedown, Sword Art Online and Knights of SPADE.


    In 2022, Solnah Labs launched their most ambitious project yet: their completed FPS experience, now renamed from NeonFPS to VectorShot. Since then, fans have been on the lookout for Solnah Labs' next move. Will there be a new IP? More features in their current games? Our only answer is to wait and see.
